The Crawling Ladybug

Made by Erika Gozar

A ladybug crawls across the screen following your cursor and leaving behind a trail of grass and flowers.

Can be used alongside Lively Wallpaper on Windows 10 or 11 as a live, interactive wallpaper.

Can also be used with New Tab Redirect on a Chrome Browser as a new tab page.
